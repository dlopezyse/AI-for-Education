### Visualization

- **[LLM Visualization](https://bbycroft.net/llm)**
  *A stunning 3D interactive walk-through of a Large Language Model architecture, showing exactly how data flows through the layers.*

- **[GAN Lab](https://poloclub.github.io/ganlab/)**
  *An interactive, browser-based experiment to understand Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs). You can train a model in real-time and visualize the "battle" between the generator and the discriminator.*
